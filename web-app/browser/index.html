Checkers
How to play:

- An uncrowned player can move only towards the other end of the board (yellow forward and red backwards)

- A piece is crowned once they reach the end of the board

- To move, first click on the piece you'd like to move and then the box you'd like to move it to, if the move is legal your token will be placed there

- To jump a piece you must click on the piece you'd like to move then click on the open space where the piece would land after you jump it

- To cancel a double jump, click above or below the square where the piece would land after its initial jump
